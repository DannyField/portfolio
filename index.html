Darkest Days [SFM]

Released in 2013, this feature length film was created using the Source Film Maker engine. Using L4D characters, I created a zombie musical heavily inspired by metalcore bands. To date it has been viewed by over 8 million people and won 'Best Animated Feature' at the 2013 'Pollygrind Film Festival' in Las Vegas.


USED : Source Film Maker, Source Engine, Adobe Suite

Rabbits

Was the cinematographer for this short film titled 'Rabbits'. Based on a true story, it tells the tale of a father and son growing up in the 1960's. Sometimes a father knows best, but sometimes it can all those away. 'Rabbits' has featured in many film festival around the world.


USED : Shot on RED 8K camera, graded in Resolve

Moire Torie GO!

The idea was simple. Create an adventure game that would allow the user to travel into the past, in any time frame and any event in time. Play with Jimi Hendrix... Or steal his guitar. The choice was yours. But trying to create such a massive experience was too much for 2 days.... I needed my own time machine


USED : Ruby 2.7.0, Colorist GEM, MyGemmy GEM

Strangers

A short form art film. Shot on Blackmagic Design 4K camera and filmed in Chongqing, China. The film is edited in such a way that when you watch it backwards overlaid with the film playing forward, a whole different story is told.


USED : Blackmagic Design 4K camera, Adobe Suite, Resolve

The Conantur
